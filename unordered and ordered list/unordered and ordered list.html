Samosa Recipe

Samosa is one of the popular snacks in India. It is my favourite snack.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 cups of cubed poiled potatoes
  • 1/2 cup of finely chopped onions
  • few cauliflower florets
  • 1/2 cup of green peas
  • 1/4 cup of chopped green coriander
  • 2 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder2-3 flour tortillas
  • 2 teaspoon red chilli powder and salt for taste
  • oil for frying

Method

  1. Take some oil in pan. Fry the onions to golden brown
  2. Put cumin and coriander and fry for another minute
  3. Add the boiled potatoes and green peas. Add salt and chilli powder according to taste. Stir for 10-15 minutes
  4. Stir in the chopped green coriander leaves and stir for another minute or so. Your filling is ready
  5. Now cut the flour tortillas lengthwise, put the filling, roll it in the shape of a triangle and seal the edges with a flour and water paste
  6. Heat some oil in deep pan. Fry the samosa to light golden brown and serve hot with mint chutney
